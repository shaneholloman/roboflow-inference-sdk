RTSP Stream Demo

This demo connects to an RTSP camera stream and processes it through a Roboflow workflow. The server captures the RTSP stream directly - no video is sent from your browser.

  • webrtc.useRtspStream() - Connect to RTSP streams
  • Server-side video capture (receive-only mode)
  • Supports credentials in URL: rtsp://user:pass@host/stream
